# 🏥 DAY 1 – FUNDAMENTALS: NURSING CONCEPTS (DETAILED)

---

# 🧠 1. WHAT IS NURSING?

### 📌 Definition (Exam-Oriented)

Nursing is a **profession focused on promoting, maintaining, and restoring health**, preventing illness, and caring for individuals, families, and communities.

👉 It includes:

* Physical care
* Emotional support
* Health education
* Rehabilitation

---

# 🎯 2. AIMS OF NURSING

* Promote health
* Prevent disease
* Restore health
* Relieve suffering
* Provide end-of-life care

---

# 🧩 3. METAPARADIGM OF NURSING (VERY IMPORTANT ⭐)

This is a **favorite NORCET question area**

### 🔑 4 Core Components:

### 1. Person

* Individual, family, or community receiving care
* Has physical, emotional, social, spiritual needs

---

### 2. Health

* State of **complete physical, mental, and social well-being**
* Not just absence of disease

---

### 3. Environment

* Internal + external surroundings affecting health
* Includes:

    * Physical (cleanliness, noise)
    * Social (family, culture)

---

### 4. Nursing

* Actions taken to care for the patient
* Includes:

    * Assessment
    * Care
    * Support

---

# 🧠 4. CHARACTERISTICS OF NURSING

* Scientific (based on knowledge)
* Art (skill + compassion)
* Holistic (whole patient care)
* Dynamic (changes with needs)
* Patient-centered

---

# 🏗️ 5. NURSING PROCESS (HIGHLY IMPORTANT ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐)

👉 This is **asked repeatedly in exams**

## 🔄 5 Steps:

### 1. Assessment

* Collect patient data
* Example: BP, symptoms

---

### 2. Diagnosis

* Identify patient problems
* Example: “Impaired breathing”

---

### 3. Planning

* Set goals
* Decide interventions

---

### 4. Implementation

* Perform nursing actions

---

### 5. Evaluation

* Check if goals are achieved

---

## 🧠 Shortcut:

👉 **ADPIE**

* A – Assessment
* D – Diagnosis
* P – Planning
* I – Implementation
* E – Evaluation

---

# ⚖️ 6. PROFESSIONAL ROLES OF A NURSE

* Caregiver
* Advocate (protect patient rights)
* Educator
* Counselor
* Manager
* Researcher

---

# 🧑‍⚕️ 7. QUALITIES OF A GOOD NURSE

* Compassion
* Communication skills
* Critical thinking
* Patience
* Responsibility

---

# 🧾 8. TYPES OF NURSING

* Clinical nursing
* Community health nursing
* Pediatric nursing
* Psychiatric nursing
* Geriatric nursing

---

# 🧭 9. HOLISTIC CARE (VERY IMPORTANT)

👉 Treat the patient as a **whole person**, not just disease.

Includes:

* Physical
* Emotional
* Social
* Spiritual care

---

# ⚖️ 10. ETHICAL PRINCIPLES IN NURSING (IMPORTANT)

### 🔑 Core Principles:

* Autonomy → Patient has right to decide
* Beneficence → Do good
* Non-maleficence → Do no harm
* Justice → Fair treatment
* Confidentiality → Protect patient info

---

# 🚨 11. LEGAL ASPECTS (BASIC)

* Informed consent
* Negligence
* Malpractice
* Documentation importance

# 🧪 DAY 1 – MCQs: NURSING CONCEPTS (WITH EXPLANATIONS)

---

## **Q1. Which of the following is NOT a component of the nursing metaparadigm?**

A. Person
B. Health
C. Treatment
D. Environment

✅ **Answer: C. Treatment**

**Explanation:**
Metaparadigm includes only:

* Person
* Health
* Environment
* Nursing

👉 “Treatment” is part of care but not a core concept.

---

## **Q2. The correct sequence of the nursing process is:**

A. Diagnosis → Assessment → Planning → Evaluation → Implementation
B. Assessment → Diagnosis → Planning → Implementation → Evaluation
C. Assessment → Planning → Diagnosis → Implementation → Evaluation
D. Planning → Assessment → Diagnosis → Evaluation → Implementation

✅ **Answer: B**

**Explanation:**
👉 Correct order = **ADPIE**

* Assessment
* Diagnosis
* Planning
* Implementation
* Evaluation

---

## **Q3. Which ethical principle refers to “doing no harm”?**

A. Autonomy
B. Justice
C. Beneficence
D. Non-maleficence

✅ **Answer: D**

**Explanation:**

* Beneficence → Do good
* Non-maleficence → **Do no harm**

---

## **Q4. A nurse respecting a patient’s decision to refuse treatment is an example of:**

A. Justice
B. Beneficence
C. Autonomy
D. Fidelity

✅ **Answer: C**

**Explanation:**
Autonomy = Patient’s right to make decisions.

---

## **Q5. Which step of the nursing process involves setting goals?**

Planning includes:

* Setting goals
