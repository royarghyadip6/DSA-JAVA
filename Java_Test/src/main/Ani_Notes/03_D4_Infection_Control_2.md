# 🧤 8. PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT (PPE) – DEEP DIVE ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

## 📌 Definition:

PPE = **protective barriers used to prevent exposure to infectious agents**

👉 Protects:

* Nurse (self-protection)
* Patient (cross-infection prevention)

---

## 🔹 TYPES OF PPE

### 1. Gloves

* Prevent hand contamination
* Types:

    * Clean (non-sterile) → routine care
    * Sterile → invasive procedures

---

### 2. Masks

#### Surgical mask:

* Protects from droplets

#### N95 mask:

* Protects from airborne infections (TB)

---

### 3. Gown / Apron

* Protects skin & clothing

---

### 4. Goggles / Face Shield

* Protects eyes from splashes

---

## 🔁 DONNING & DOFFING (VERY IMPORTANT)

### 🟢 Donning (Putting ON):

1. Hand hygiene
2. Gown
3. Mask
4. Goggles
5. Gloves

---

### 🔴 Doffing (Removing):

1. Gloves
2. Goggles
3. Gown
4. Mask
5. Hand hygiene

---

## ⚠️ COMMON ERRORS:

* Touching outer contaminated surface
* Wrong removal order
* Skipping hand hygiene

---

## 🎯 EXAM FOCUS:

* PPE order
* N95 vs surgical mask
* When to use gloves

# 🧪 9. STERILIZATION & DISINFECTION (DETAILED)

---

## 🔥 STERILIZATION

### 📌 Definition:

Complete destruction of **ALL microorganisms including spores**

---

### 🔹 METHODS:

#### 1. Moist Heat (Autoclave)

* Steam under pressure
* Most effective

---

#### 2. Dry Heat

* Hot air oven

---

#### 3. Chemical

* Ethylene oxide

---

#### 4. Radiation

* UV / gamma rays

---

## 🧴 DISINFECTION

### 📌 Definition:

Destruction of **most microorganisms (NOT spores)**

---

## 🔹 LEVELS:

### High-Level

* Kills most organisms except spores

### Intermediate

* Kills bacteria + viruses

### Low-Level

* Kills some microbes

---

## ⚠️ DIFFERENCE:

| Sterilization   | Disinfection |
|-----------------|--------------|
| Kills all       | Kills most   |
| Includes spores | No spores    |

---

## 🎯 EXAM TRAPS:

* Autoclave = sterilization
* Disinfection ≠ sterilization

---

# 🗑️ 10. BIOMEDICAL WASTE MANAGEMENT (VERY IMPORTANT ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐)

---

## 📌 Definition:

Safe handling, segregation, transport, and disposal of hospital waste

---

## 🎨 COLOR CODING (HIGH-YIELD)

### 🟡 Yellow:

* Human tissue, body parts

---

### 🔴 Red:

* Contaminated plastic (IV sets, tubing)

---

### 🔵 Blue:

* Glass (vials, bottles)

---

### ⚪ White (Translucent):

* Sharps (needles, blades)

---

## ⚠️ KEY PRINCIPLES:

* Segregation at source
* Use color-coded bins
* No mixing

---

## ❗ COMMON ERRORS:

* Mixing waste
* Recapping needles

---

# 🚫 11. ISOLATION PRECAUTIONS (DETAILED)

---

## 📌 Purpose:

Prevent spread of infection

---

## 🔹 TYPES:

---

### 1. Contact Precautions

👉 For direct/indirect contact infections

* Gloves + gown

---

### 2. Droplet Precautions

👉 Large droplets

* Surgical mask

---

### 3. Airborne Precautions

👉 Small particles (TB)

* N95 mask
* Negative pressure room

---

## 🎯 KEY POINT:

* TB → Airborne
* COVID/Flu → Droplet

# 🏥 12. HOSPITAL-ACQUIRED INFECTION (HAI)

---

## 📌 Definition:

Infection acquired **after 48 hours of hospital admission**

---

## 🔹 COMMON TYPES:

* UTI (catheter-related)
* Pneumonia (ventilator-associated)
* Surgical site infection

---

## 🔹 CAUSES:

* Poor hygiene
* Improper sterilization
* Long hospital stay

---

## 🔹 PREVENTION:

* Hand hygiene
* Sterile technique
* PPE use

# 💉 14. POST-EXPOSURE PROPHYLAXIS (PEP)

---

## 📌 Definition:

Immediate treatment after exposure to infection

---

## 🔹 COMMON EXPOSURES:

* Needle-stick injury
* Blood/body fluid exposure

---

## 🔹 STEPS (VERY IMPORTANT):

1. Wash area immediately
2. Do NOT squeeze wound
3. Report incident
4. Start prophylaxis
5. Follow-up testing

---

## 🔹 EXAMPLES:

* HIV exposure → ART drugs
* Hepatitis → vaccine/immunoglobulin

---

## ⚠️ TIME CRITICAL:

👉 Start within **1–2 hours**

---

# 🎯 HIGH-YIELD SUMMARY

* PPE order (donning/doffing)
* Autoclave = sterilization
* Waste color coding
* Isolation types (contact/droplet/airborne)
* HAI definition
* PEP steps

---

# 🧠 FINAL QUICK REVISION

* PPE = barrier protection
* Sterilization = kills ALL
* Waste = color coded
* Isolation = 3 types
* HAI = after 48 hrs
* PEP = immediate action
